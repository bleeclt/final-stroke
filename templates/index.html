Don't Stroke Out!

Act F.A.S.T!


Did you know that 80% of Strokes can be prevented? This can be done by acting F.A.S.T at the first signs of a Stroke, but it can also be prevented by changing your health risks. Some major factors leading to Strokes are Geographic Location, Gender, Race, Marriage Status, Hypertension, Heart Disease, Glucose Levels, and Smoking Habits. If you would like to calculate your probability of having a stroke please visit our Calculator tab and enter your current information.
